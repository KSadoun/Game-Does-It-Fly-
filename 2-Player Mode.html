Does It Fly? Made To You By Me

Game Rules:

  • Write the names of the players in the given box below
  • For player 1(upper side) contols are alt "a" and alt "s" for true and false respectively
  • For player 2(lower side) controls are alt "k" and alt "l" for true and false respectively
  • A word will appear in the center and you will click true if the word is something that flies, if it doesn't fly click false, the word will change when anyone clicks on true or false buttons
  • Your score increases with every correct answer and decreases with every wrong answer
  • The person who scores 10 first wins the game
